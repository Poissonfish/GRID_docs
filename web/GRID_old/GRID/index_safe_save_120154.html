Application of Unmanned Aerial Vehicles(UAVs) in agricultural use began in 2016, and it brings the “big data” era for the industry. With the benefit from the development of AI in image identification, extracting information from the high-throughput images has a full application in the industry. Such as weeds identification, plant species recognition, growing phase determination, plant disease detection, the crop produces prediction, seeds’ nutrition prediction, et cetera. The design purpose of GridFree is to improve the efficiency of manually labeling plants or seeds images. GridFree labels images at the pixel level, it does not need any image pattern information. It uses a divide-and-combine strategy to separate image pixels into groups and identify the items automatically. GitHub
